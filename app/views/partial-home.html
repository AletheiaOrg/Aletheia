Welcome To Personify

This application was built using Personify.js, a JavaScript-based library that allows easy access to IBM Watson services and Twitter data.


This Application

Extracts cognitive and social characteristics, including Big Five Personality Traits, Values, and Needs, from communications data provided by Twitter.


What Does This App Do?

When you enter a search term, you'll get back Big Five personality traits from a collective group of tweets on that topic from a specified US state. You'll find out which traits stand out in people discussing the topic. You'll also receive data on how those traits compare to the US in general. What are the traits of people talking about the latest news headline? Your company's products? Your favorite brands?
